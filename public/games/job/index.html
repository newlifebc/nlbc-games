The Suffering of Job

This game is similar to the game of Mafia, but is about the suffering of Job.

Reference:

Book of Job. Specifically chapters 1, 2, and 42.

A good guy named Job lived in Uz. He has 7 sons and 3 daughters. He had 7000 sheep, 3000 camels, 500 oxen, 500 donkeys. He was the richest person in the area. First Satan burned all his sheep. Second they stole the camels and killed his servants. Third his children all died in a windstorm. Fourth he got boils all over his body.

Summary:

Gameplay:

Age Range: 8 to 15
Number of Participants: 5 to 25
Equipment:
  1. A place to play
Roles:
  • Job - tries to figure out who is doing this to him
  • God - selects Satan and Job and allows Satan to destroy stuff
  • Satan - chooses what to destroy
  • Job's Family, Livestock, Possessions - get destroyed
Mapping to Mafia:
Job Role Mafia Role
God Judge
Satan Murderer/Spy
Job Detective
Job's Family, Livestock, Possessions Victims
Rules:
  • The oldest person is 'God'
  • Everyone closes their eyes except for 'God'. 'God' taps someone once to be Job. 'God' taps someone twice to be Satan.
  • Everyone closes their eyes and 'God' says "Satan choose to destroy one of Job's possessions"
  • Satan wakes up and chooses someone to destroy.
  • Satan goes back to sleep
  • Everyone wakes up
  • 'God' announces who has been destroyed

Questions:

  • Why did Satan have to ask God for permission to test Job? Satan needs an audience. Satan wanted to prove to God that even Job would foresake God if his stuff were taken away.
  • Why did God allow Satan to kill Job's family? What if the rest of Job's family were good people too?
